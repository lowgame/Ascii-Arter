Why this one changed

Less SaaS template. More atmosphere.

Instead of cramming product cards into the first screen, RelayStack now uses the package for pure brand mood: wave motion, giant wordmark and a front page that feels closer to Apple-style launch pages than a standard growth SaaS clone.

What you ship

One JSON per section. One mount call per zone.

Design the scene in the editor, export the JSON, and mount it inside a hero or CTA wrapper. The rest is normal HTML/CSS.
